Frequently Asked Questions

The Certified Management Accountant (CMA) is a globally recognised professional certification issued by the Institute of Management Accountants (IMA), USA. It validates expertise in financial planning, analysis, control, decision support, and professional ethics — recognised by employers in over 100 countries.
You need: (1) a Bachelor's degree from an accredited university, (2) IMA membership (we assist with this at no extra cost), and (3) two continuous years of professional experience in management accounting or financial management — which can be completed within seven years of passing the exam.
The full programme covers both CMA Parts over 6 months and 180 hours of training. Part 1 runs for the first 3 months; Part 2 for the following 3 months. Sessions run on weekday evenings and weekends — both classroom (JLT Dubai) and live online options are available every cohort.
Your enrolment includes HOCK International premium study materials — the gold standard for CMA preparation. This covers the full textbook set for both parts, HOCK's question bank (3,000+ practice questions), mock exams, and online resources, all specifically aligned to the IMA exam blueprint.
You can attend all classes again, completely free of charge, for the relevant part. There is no limit on how many times you can repeat. This is a commitment we make at enrolment — your success is our success.
